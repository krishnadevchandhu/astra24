About ASTRA2024!

Department of Computer Science, Sacred Heart College (Autonomous), Thevara, organises an Inter Collegiate IT Fest, ASTRA 2024 on December 11th and 12th 2024. ‘ASTRA 2024’ is an intellectual merge of events to explore the technical aspects of students. It allows them to exhibit their skill, ability, and talent. It aims at bringing out the talent in students and honours them for what they are.
